Ediki Bekoli Development and Cultural Organization

Yes!! OKorikori !!! Motomoko (Together - we are one man)

Objectives

To promote the culture of the Mbonge people to all its denizens.

To foster development in the village: provision of pipe-borne water, electricity, and construction of farm-to-market roads.

Providing scholarships to children of school-going age at all levels.

Assisting petty businesses for widows and orphans.

Supporting children ready to learn a trade.

Purpose

Limit poverty to the nearest minimum.

Encourage and support education at all levels through a five-year development plan.

Organize and buttress yearly cultural festivals.
